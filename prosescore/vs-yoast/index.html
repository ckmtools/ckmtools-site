Why choose ProseScore over Yoast readability

Works anywhere, no WordPress needed ProseScore runs in any browser. Analyze blog drafts, documentation, emails, or marketing copy without installing a CMS.
8 formulas vs 1 Yoast uses Flesch Reading Ease as its only readability metric. ProseScore runs 8 formulas and averages them into a consensus grade for a more reliable score.
Runs offline, keeps your text private ProseScore runs entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to a server. Yoast requires a WordPress installation with server-side processing.
Free tier is generous Get Flesch-Kincaid, word count, and reading time for free. Pro unlocks all 8 formulas, sentiment, keywords, and SEO scoring for $3.99/mo.

When to use each

Use ProseScore for standalone content analysis If you write content outside WordPress — docs, emails, articles, marketing copy — ProseScore gives you readability, sentiment, keywords, and SEO scoring in one tool.
Use Yoast for WordPress-integrated SEO If your content lives in WordPress and you need on-page SEO features (meta tags, sitemaps, schema markup) alongside readability, Yoast integrates directly into the editor.
